Compound Workouts

Push-Ups

1. Muscles worked: Chest, shoulders, triceps, core, back.
2. Form tip: Straight body, elbows at 45Â°.

Pull-Ups

1. Muscles worked: Lats, biceps, shoulders, core, back.
2. Form tip: Full extension, pull chest up.

Barbell Squats

1. Muscles worked: Quads, hamstrings, glutes, core, calves.
2. Form tip: Straight back, knees aligned, heels.

Deadlift

1. Muscles worked: Hamstrings, glutes, lower back, core.
2. Form tip: Straight back, engage core, lift.

Romanian Deadlift

1. Muscles worked: Hamstrings, glutes, lower back, core.
2. Form tip: Hinge at hips, straight back.

Barbell Chest Press

1. Muscles worked: Chest, shoulders, triceps, core, back.
2. Form tip: Keep feet flat, controlled movement.

Barbell Overhead Press

1. Muscles worked: Shoulders, triceps, upper chest, core.
2. Form tip: Keep core tight, straight back.

Isolation Workouts

Cable Chest Fly

1. Muscles worked: Chest, shoulders, triceps, core.
2. Form tip: Slight bend, control movement, squeeze.

Tricep Pushdown

1. Muscles worked: Triceps, shoulders, forearms.
2. Form tip: Keep elbows close, controlled motion.

Cable overhead triceps

1. Muscles worked: Triceps, shoulders, upper arms.
2. Form tip: Keep elbows stable, fully extend arms.

T-Bar Rows

1. Muscles worked: Lats, rhomboids, traps, biceps, shoulders.
2. Form tip: Keep chest up, retract shoulder blades.

Preacher Curl

1. Muscles worked: Biceps, forearms.
2. Form tip: Keep elbows fixed, control movement.

Barbell shrugs

1. Muscles worked: Traps, shoulders, upper back.
2. Form tip: Lift shoulders straight up, avoid rolling.

Leg Extension

1. Muscles worked: Quadriceps, hip flexors.
2. Form tip: Extend fully, avoid locking knee.

Leg Press

1. Muscles worked: Quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, calves.
2. Form tip: Keep feet flat, don't lock knees.

Calf Raises

1. Muscles worked: Calves, soleus, gastrocnemius.
2. Form tip: Full extension, slow controlled descent.
